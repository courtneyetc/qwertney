Courtney Christopher

Designer in tech · Internet folklorist

An award-winning designer and visual artist based in California who is passionate about Internet culture and history, Courtney has 15 years of professional experience in the US and Asia, from FAANG companies to start-ups. As of December 2024, Courtney works as a Staff Designer at Reddit.
